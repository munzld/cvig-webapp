Chippewa Valley Intergroup (CVIG) is a recognized part of Alcoholics Anonymous (AA) that parallels and complements the AA Conference service structure. CVIG is dedicated to helping the still suffering alcoholic to find a solution.

If you are struggling with a drinking problem and would like to speak to someone please give us a call: 715-835-5543 or 800-471-9876. We maintain a hotline staffed by volunteers - all of whom are recovering alcoholics.

Alcoholics Anonymous (AA) is completely non-professional and is simply a fellowship of men and women who are focused on recovery from alcoholism.

Join Us! Monthly Intergroup Meeting

The Chippewa Valley Intergroup meets the fourth Wednesday of each month at 6:00pm at Club 12, 2906 Pomona Drive, Eau Claire. We would love to have you join us!
